# 📊 Brazilian E-commerce Data Analysis (Olist Dataset)

## 🔍 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a real-world Brazilian e-commerce dataset to understand how customers behave, how orders flow through the system, and where business inefficiencies exist.

The goal wasn’t just to “build dashboards” — it was to approach the data like a real analyst: clean it, question it, and extract insights that actually matter.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Identify revenue trends over time
* Analyze delivery performance and delays
* Evaluate payment methods and transaction behavior
* Explore customer satisfaction using review scores

---

## 🧰 Tools Used

* **SQL** → Data extraction, joins, business queries
* **Python (EDA)** → Data cleaning and exploration
* **Excel** → Initial inspection and quick validation
* **Power BI** → Dashboard and visualization

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was not clean out of the box — and that’s where most people fail.

Key steps:

* Handled missing values in delivery and approval dates
* Fixed inconsistent timestamps and formats
* Validated relationships between orders, payments, and customers
* Removed or treated irrelevant/null-heavy fields

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue & Order Trends

* Tracked daily and monthly revenue patterns
* Identified peak sales periods

### 2. Delivery Performance

* Measured delivery time vs estimated delivery
* Identified delays and logistics inefficiencies

### 3. Customer Distribution

* Analyzed geographic spread of customers
* Found high-demand regions

### 4. Payment Behavior

* Compared usage of credit cards, vouchers, etc.
* Evaluated transaction values across methods

### 5. Customer Satisfaction

* Linked review scores with delivery performance
* Identified factors driving low ratings

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

The Power BI dashboard provides:

* Revenue trends over time
* Delivery delay analysis
* Customer distribution map
* Payment method breakdown
* Review score insights

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Delivery delays directly impact customer satisfaction
* Certain regions consistently show higher order volumes
* Credit cards dominate transactions, but other methods show niche patterns
* Revenue trends show clear seasonal spikes
